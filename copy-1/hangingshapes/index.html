Echoes_of_Evolution

About

Echoes of Evolution was a 2.5D side-scrolling game developed during a Nostra-hosted game jam with the theme "Evolution." In this platformer, players use arrows to defeat evolved monsters while navigating through dynamic environments. The objective is to collect evolution potions, enabling the player to adapt and evolve based on the environment's challenges. With its engaging mechanics and thematic alignment, the game highlighted the creative collaboration and quick problem-solving of our team.
